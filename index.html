Get Started

This site allows teachers to create and issue badges, and allows students to claim badges they've earned.

So what is a badge anyway? Digital badges are a way to display your accomplisments online. You can earn badges from many different places, and they can be displayed in different ways, including in your Mozilla Badge Backpack.

To start, you need to indicate if you want to ISSUE a badge, or CLAIM a badge.

Issuers start by creating a badge. To create a badge, you'll need to provide information about who you are, what the badge is for, and a link to a page indicating the sort of criteria are required to complete the badge (a Google Doc, for instance). Once you've created your badge, you can issue it to your students if they've met the criteria; then those students can come to the site to claim their badge. Scroll down to start creating!

To claim your badge, you'll need to enter your email address to find badges that have been issued to you. You can then add them to your Mozilla Badge Backpack. If you haven't yet registered with Mozilla to create a backpack, you'll have the opportunity to do so. Claim your badge now!
